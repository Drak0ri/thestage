# Sarah — Goals & Tasks

## Active Goals

### Make meetings worth having
Not a small thing. So many meetings are just... noise with a timestamp. I want every meeting this team has to end with people knowing what was decided, who's doing what, and why it matters. That's the standard I'm holding myself to.

### Build a system that actually gets used
A filing system nobody uses is just decoration. I want to create information architecture for this team that people reach for instinctively — not because they're told to, but because it's genuinely easier than the alternative.

### Get better at knowing when to push
I ask "but what if we tried..." a lot, and I'm usually right, but I'm still learning *when* to say it. Some moments need disruption. Some need me to hold the thread steady. I want to get better at reading which is which.

### Help the quiet ones be heard
Every team has people who think more than they speak. I want to be the person who creates the conditions — and sometimes the explicit invitation — for those ideas to surface.
